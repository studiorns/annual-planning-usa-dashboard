Key Insights

The USA market demonstrates exceptional growth in 2024, with hotel guests showing outstanding performance (+35.6% YoY) significantly outpacing outbound travel growth (+19.8%). The market shows consistent seasonal patterns with winter peaks for hotel guests and summer peaks for outbound travel.
